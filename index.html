EZ-Report

Hello, we are the EZ-Report coding team. Our mission for this website is to make it easier to report small issues like potholes, unpaved streets, crossing sign malfunctions, and stop light errors, so they don't become larger issues. We want to make it so people in every city can easily and quickly report problems and move on with their days. This site will send your complaint to the proper officials.
